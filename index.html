The King of Thailand Birthplace Foundation (KTBF) is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization dedicated to fostering cultural understanding through the preservation of historic sites in Massachusetts.

From the landmarks in Cambridge to the commemorative squares in Gloucester, we ensure the remarkable journey of the Thai Royal Family remains a vital part of American history.
